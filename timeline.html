My Time in Activism

Communications Director, East Bay Young Democrats

2017-Present

As Communications Director, I work with the EBYD Executive Board on crafting our message and our policy direction, both for our membership and externally. In this role I've spoken on issues like Alameda's Sanctuary City proposal, marched in the Women's March, and worked with housing activists to keep EBYD up-to-date on the state of housing in our region.

Communications Chair, City of Alameda Democratic Club

2017-Present

CADC is focused on bringing high-quality programs and events to our membership, as well making our presence known in Alameda City issues. In this role, I work with the CADC Programs Chairs and Events Chair to organize and promote upcoming events to our membership.

Founding Member, East Bay Forward

2016-Present

East Bay Forward spun out of the San Francisco YIMBY movement to be a voice for housing development and housing justice in the East Bay. It was as a member of East Bay Forward that my passion for housing activism was stoked, and as a member of this group I have spoken before several local governments on behalf of housing growth.

Chair, Crown Student Senate

2009-2011

As the Chair for Crown Student Senate, I led the Senate for Crown College. We were responsible for over $100k in budgets and endowments, which we used to support causes and events on-campus and in the community. My focus as Chair was to run meetings efficiently and fairly, making sure that everyone's voice was heard and we were bringing in community members who had different backgrounds.

Senator, Crown Student Senate

2007-2011

The Crown Student Senate is responsible for representing the Crown student body to the larger campus community, as well as administrating over $100k in student funds in the form of budgets and endowments. During my time as a Senator, I made a point of representing my fellow students at other campus cuases and clubs, trying to connect align our goals with the goals of our community as a whole.
